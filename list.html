上下四方建筑事务所(SITUATE)于2018年秋在北京成立，是一个多元化的设计研究工作室。


上下四方(SITUATE)致力于场所空间的探索与研究。空间因人而造，我们关注人在空间中的体验。在设计实践中，我们相信空间应根植于地域，回应当下；空间应与自然有机结合，和谐共生；空间应作用于精神，为人建立认同与归属。


上下四方(SITUATE)认为建造活动是社会资源整合与再分配的过程，我们的工作内容也依此展开。在项目实践中，我们深入研究资源背景，平衡其功能、经济、文化等因素，挖掘项目的独特性和可能性，以此提出最优解决方案。


上下四方(SITUATE)作品多次被世界专业媒体报道，设计实践涵盖建筑与室内、展览与装置、城市与景观等领域。

Founded and based in Beijing in the autumn of 2018, SITUATE Architecture is a multi-disciplinary design and research atelier.


SITUATE Architecture is committed to the research and practice on place and space. We believe that space reacts to its context and should be in harmony with nature; space is made for people and should contribute to spiritual life. With the value above, we keep experimenting with the spatial experiences which help to create the sense of being and belonging.


SITUATE Architecture takes building as a process of social resource integration and redistribution. In the practice of a project, we take comprehensive analysis on its background balancing all the factors of function, economy and culture. On that basis we explore the possibility and uniqueness of a project and look for an optimized solution to it.


The works of SITUATE Architecture range from architecture and interior, exhibition and installation to urbanization and landscape etc., many of which have been featured by global professional media.
